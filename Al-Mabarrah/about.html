our history

The late Amir Sheikh Sabah Al-Salem Al-Mubarak Al-Sabah, may God have mercy on him, established this foundation in 1976 with the aim of supporting and encouraging outstanding students to pursue their studies in the stages of higher education and advanced scientific research. Academically and technically in the State of Kuwait and other countries and support promising future generations regardless of religion, race or nationality. Therefore, this foundation aims to promote and encourage the academic prosperity of students in Kuwait and abroad in the context of their quest to achieve excellence in gaining knowledge, carrying out practical research, innovation and advanced thought in the fields of science. Literature and the arts.

  • Scholarships and grants to outstanding students to conduct advanced research and postgraduate studies in various fields.

  • Supporting the Kuwait Society for the Handicapped, the squash tournament, and the Kuwait Red Crescent.

  • Supporting, sponsoring and honoring the Arab creativity initiative in its annual ceremony for the ninth session hosted by the State of Kuwait in 2018 at Jaber Cultural Center under the patronage of Sheikh Sabah Al-Salem Al-Mubarak Al-Sabah (may God have mercy on him).

  • Constructing a building in the name of the late Sheikh Sabah Al-Salem Al-Sabah in the Scientific Club.

Al-Mabarrah Objectives

  • Support and encourage outstanding students to pursue higher education.

  • Providing scholarships and incentives for researchers in all fields.

  • Presenting appreciation awards to outstanding scholars, writers and artists.
